About this planner

Campus Life Planner helps you track assignments, study sessions, and club events in one place — due dates, durations, and tags, all searchable with live regex filtering. Everything is saved to your browser's local storage, so your planner is ready the next time you open this page.

How it works

  • Add a task with a title, due date, duration, and tag.
  • Search records using plain text or a regular expression.
  • Sort by due date, title, or duration.
  • Track your weekly time cap on the Dashboard.
  • Export your data as JSON, or import a JSON file to restore it.
